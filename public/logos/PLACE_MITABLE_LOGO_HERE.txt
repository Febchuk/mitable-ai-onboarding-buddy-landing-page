This is where your Mitable logos should go:

Required files:
- mitable-logo.png (Main horizontal logo, 200x60px)
- mitable-logo-white.png (White version for dark backgrounds)  
- mitable-icon.png (Square icon version, 64x64px)
- favicon.ico (Browser favicon, multiple sizes)

The logos are currently SVG placeholders in the config file.
Replace the SVG code in src/lib/config.tsx with image references like:
<img src="/assets/logos/mitable-logo.png" alt="Mitable AI" />
